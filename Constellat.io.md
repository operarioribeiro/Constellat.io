# [Constellat.io](https://operarioribeiro.github.com/Constellat.io)

> _"Ad astra humanitas."_

## Open Project:

**Constellat.io** is an [open science](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open_science) [social media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Social_media) where each [stellar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Star) [constellation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constellation) has its own  [open research](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open_research) [working group](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Working_group) and co-decide and co-create a [collaborative](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Collaborative_intelligence) their [artistic model diorama](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diorama) [virtual world](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Virtual_world) with [documentation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Software_documentation) and care for it like a [virtual pet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Virtual_pet).

## Open Finances:

**Constellat.io profits** are to be all donated to the [International Space Station (ISS)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Space_Station).
